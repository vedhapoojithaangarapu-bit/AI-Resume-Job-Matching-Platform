Senior Frontend Engineer
InnovateTech Solutions — Hybrid (Austin, TX)
Full-Time Position

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are seeking a Senior Frontend Engineer to join our core product team. You will own the client-side architecture of our flagship web platform and collaborate closely with product managers.

WHAT YOU'LL DO
* Architect, develop, and maintain responsive web applications using React v18 and Next.js.
* Optimize application performance for maximum speed and scalability.
* Mentor junior developers and participate in code reviews to ensure high code quality.

WHAT YOU MUST BRING
* Minimum of 5+ years of professional software engineering experience.
* Masterful command of core web fundamentals: HTML5, CSS3, and modern JavaScript (ES6+).
* Proven experience building highly interactive user interfaces.
* Exceptional problem-solving skills and a collaborative team-player mindset.

NICE TO HAVE
* Experience working with state management libraries like Redux Toolkit.
* Familiarity with Docker containerization and AWS deployments.
* A Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related technical field.

WHAT WE OFFER
* Competitive salary with annual performance bonus.
* Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance coverage.
* Unlimited Paid Time Off (PTO).
